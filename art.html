JIG-SAW

I designed and made this play house for my daughter the year she was born. It is a two storey structure built to adult proportions allowing grown-ups to stand 'upstairs'.

It also served as a back-drop for an album cover. To keep with albums theme I painted it as a continuous jig-saw.

PAGES DRESS

This dress was commissioned by composer Julie Feeney. It is fashioned out of paper, the orchestral scores from her album 'Pages'.

I designed and made it in 3 parts, the corsetted collar, corset-top and skirt. Many nights went without sleep as I'd an extremely tight deadline and all the stitching I did by hand.
